## How to Get the Best Support

### Before Reaching Out
- Check our [Knowledge Base](/knowledge-base) for common solutions
- Review the [Documentation](/docs) for detailed guidance
- Search the community forum for similar questions

### When Contacting Support
- **Be Specific**: Describe your issue in detail, including error messages
- **Provide Context**: Share relevant configuration details or screenshots
- **Include Steps**: List what you've already tried to resolve the issue
- **Environment Details**: Mention your system, browser, or app version

## Response Times

| Support Channel | Response Time | Availability |
|----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Live Chat | Immediate | Mon-Fri, 9AM-5PM PST |
| Email Support | Within 24 hours | 24/7 |
| Community Forum | Varies | 24/7 |

## Emergency Support

For critical production issues that require immediate attention, please:
1. Contact us via email with "URGENT" in the subject line
2. Follow up with live chat if available
3. Provide detailed information about the impact and urgency
